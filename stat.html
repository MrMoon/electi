Div. 2 Readiness Score ($D_R$) Equation

DR = Score from Max Rating + Score from Avg Contest Rating + Score from Contest Count + Score from Internal Contest + Score from Weighted Solved Problems - Recency Penalty - Skipped Penalty

Where:

  • Score from Max Rating: min(60, (UserMaxRating / 2000) ⋅ 60)
  • Score from Avg Contest Rating: min(50, (AverageContestRating / 1800) ⋅ 50)
  • Score from Contest Count: min(20, (ContestParticipationCount / 100) ⋅ 20)
  • Score from Internal Contest: (InternalContestPerformanceScore / 200) ⋅ 30)
  • Score from Weighted Solved Problems: min(40, (RawWeightedSolvedProblemScore / 200) ⋅ 40)
    *Raw score is calculated as: (Problems < 1400 rating ⋅ 1) + (Problems 1400-1799 rating ⋅ 2) + (Problems ≥ 1800 rating ⋅ 3)
  • Recency Penalty: min(40, (DaysSinceLastAttempt / 90) ⋅ 40)
  • Skipped Penalty: min(20, (SkippedSubmissionsCount / 50) ⋅ 20)

*The final $D_R$ score is capped between 0 and 200. Penalties are always non-negative.
